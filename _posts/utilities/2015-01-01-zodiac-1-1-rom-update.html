--- layout: post title: "Zodiac 1.1 Rom Update" categories: utilities --- The Zodiac 1.1 ROM Update is vital if you want to play any games. The update added new features such as background music and a lot of bug fixes. To check if you need it, go to the home screen and bring up the menu. Tap Options > About Home. If it says 1.1 then you do not need this update.
